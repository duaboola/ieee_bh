ICETZC 2025

We are thrilled to announce the upcoming International Conference on Energy Transition and Net Zero Carbon, scheduled to take place from February 2nd to February 5th, 2025, in the Kingdom of Bahrain. As global leaders in the pursuit of sustainable energy solutions, this conference promises to be a pivotal event in shaping the future of our planet's energy landscape. With a focus on fostering dialogue, collaboration, and innovation, participants can anticipate engaging discussions, insightful presentations, and actionable strategies aimed at achieving net-zero carbon emissions. While our website is currently under construction, we invite you to stay tuned for further updates and details regarding this landmark event. Together, let us embark on a journey towards a greener, more sustainable future.
